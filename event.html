Desert Hyperloop Competition

This competition, launched by the Young Architect Competitions, aims at designing a new test center for the Hyperloop technology in the heart of the Nevada desert, a few kilometers away from Las Vegas. ​A cash prize of € 15,000 will be awarded to the winners selected by an outstanding jury panel made of renowned personalities such as ​Kazuyo Sejima​, Winy Maas, Ben van Berkel, Nicola Scaranaro, ​Carlo Ratt​i and many more.

Indian hyperloop pod competition

Indian Hyperloop Pod Competition or IHPC, hosted by IIT madras, is the first Hyperloop pod competition to be held in India. It will be a global competition, attracting Hyperloop teams from across the world to compete. It is expected to be conducted in early 2021. With this competition, the Indian student community embarks on the journey of promoting and bringing successful amends to this technology. Hyperloop IITB is preparing for the competition and will start working on the design as soon as the final dates of the competition are released.

SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition

This is an annual competition organized by SpaceX, inviting university students and independant engineering teams to design and build the best Hyperloop pod. It is a two part contest. The first part of the contest is Design Weekend, which is held at Texas A&M University. Teams showcase the Hyperloop pod designs which are judged by SpaceX engineers and university professors. Selected teams then move onto the final phase of the competition where they have the opportunity to test their pods on a one-mile Hyperloop test track (will be made a 10 mile track soon) near the SpaceX headquarters in Hawthorne, California.
